Change feeds

For various reasons (e.g. they've forgotten their password, not happy with the name for their blog etc.) students may wish to change the feed they have already registered.

Selecting this option, allows them to change their registered feeds.

Students have to be aware that if they change their feed, then any content from the old registered feed is lost. BIM only maintains a copy of the currently registered feed. Essentially, students have to remember to post all the content already on their old feed, into the new one. Typically not a big problem as students usually change their blogs before posting too much content.
