Mark posts

This page displays details about all of your students who have registered their blog/feed with this activity. The details include:

  • Username in Moodle;

    Click on the username to send the student an email.

  • Name and blog;

    The students name is a link to the their external blog/feed.

  • Questions;

    Which shows two bits of information:

    1. Number of questions answered by the student;

      Will show something like 3 of 4. This means that there are 4 questions in total for students to answer and this student appears to have answered 3.

    2. Total number of posts and link to Allocate posts.

      The number of all the posts copied by this activity from the student's blog/feed. For example, 9 posts.

      The total number of posts is also a link to a page that allows you to view all of the student's posts on one page and change the allocation of posts to questions.

  • Marked.

    Shows the number of posts allocated to questions and how many of them have been marked. For example, 1 of 3 indicates that the 3 of the student's posts have been allocated to questions and that 1 of these 3 have been marked.

The questions

There may be a number of additional columns in the displayed table. But only if your BIM activity has questions created. There will be one additional column for each question.

The colum for each question will either show:

  • No answer; or

    This means that either the student has not answerd this question or that the post in which they did answer the question has not been automatically allocated to the question. If automatic allocation fails, then you have to use the manual allocation service provided in the Questions column described above.

  • Details about the post.

Details about the post

The details shown will include:

  • Live blog;

    A link to the post on the student's blog/feed.

  • status.

    What is displayed depends on the status of the post. Possibilities include:

    • Mark it - indicating the post has not been marked yet.
    • (3.5 - Marked) - indicating the post has been marked and given the mark of 3.5. Marked posts are not visible to students.
    • (4.0 - Released) - indicating that the post was awarded a mark of 4 and has been released to the student.
    • SUSPENDED - indicating that the post has been suspended so that it cannot be released to the student.

    The status will always be a link to a page where you can modify the status of the post. This is where you mark a post, make comments on it and suspend the post.
