Registered details

To contribute to a BIM activity, students must register their blog/feed. The registerd details table shows various details about these students and their blog/feed.

The details include:

  • Username and name as stored in Moodle.
  • A link to their email address.
  • The total number of posts BIM has copied from the student's blog/feed.
  • The time since the student last made a post.
  • The ability to view or change the student's current blog/feed.

    IMPORTANT: If you change a student's current blog/feed, then all current information about the student's posts - including any marks and comments you have made - will be deleted.
