Your students

There are two main pages provide as part of "Your students":

  1. View student details; and

    Provides a list of all your students including those who have and have not registered their blog/feed. On this page you can:

    • View details about the students and their blogs and posts.
    • Send email to the students.
    • Register or change the student's blog/feed.
  2. Mark posts;

    If your BIM activity has set questions, this page allows you to:

    • Manually allocate student posts to questions; and
    • Mark student posts - including suspending posts.
